Service

At Srinivas Institute of Technology, we are committed to providing a range of services that support the academic, personal, and professional growth of our students. Our comprehensive services are designed to enhance the overall student experience and ensure their success. Academic Support: We offer a variety of academic support services, including tutoring, writing assistance, and study skills workshops. Our goal is to help students achieve their academic potential and excel in their studies. Career Services: Our dedicated career services team provides guidance and resources to help students prepare for their future careers. This includes resume building, interview preparation, job search strategies, and connections with potential employers through job fairs and networking events. Counseling and Wellness: We prioritize the mental and emotional well-being of our students. Our counseling services offer confidential support for a range of personal issues, while our wellness programs promote a healthy and balanced lifestyle. Library and Research Services: Our state-of-the-art library offers extensive resources, including books, journals, databases, and digital media. Students have access to research assistance and various workshops to enhance their research skills. Technology Services: We provide a range of technology services to support students' learning experiences. This includes computer labs, software access, technical support, and online learning platforms. Student Life Services: We offer a variety of student life services, including housing, dining, transportation, and campus safety. Our goal is to create a supportive and vibrant campus environment where students can thrive. International Student Services: Our dedicated international student services team assists international students with their unique needs, including visa support, cultural orientation, and integration into the college community. Disability Services: We are committed to providing an inclusive environment for all students. Our disability services ensure that students with disabilities have access to the necessary accommodations and support to succeed academically and personally.
